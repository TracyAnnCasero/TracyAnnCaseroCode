SMDC Grass Residences Complex where SMDC Fern is located completes the full integration of this exciting and dynamic lifestyle district in the Quezon City. Built with an exclusive bridge way connected to SM City North Edsa, residents can enjoy instant access to all the good things offered in the lifestyle district that only one can get while living in a Grass Residences unit. Enjoy 800 retail shops, over 200 restaurant and dining options, 12 cinemas including an IMAX theatre

Enjoy your very own private amenity area decked with a sprawling playground, picnic areas, and swimming pools.


Location

SMDC Grass and Fern Residences are located in Quezon City, connected to SM City North Edsa via bridge way. It’s definitely a convenient, exciting and entertaining place to come home to. It is also situated in a highly accessible area. It is right by main roads both for going to the North and South side of the metro. There is an abundance of options for public transportations like jeepney, bus and van terminals. It is also near both MRT and LRT stations for your convenience. Private transport is also a breeze for vehicle owners.
